Simple Indomie Recipe

Prep time - 5 mins | Cook time - 3-5 mins | Servings - 1 plate | Difficulty - Easy

Ingredients

  • One or two packets of Indomie noodles (any flavour you prefer)
  • One or two eggs
  • One medium onion
  • One medium tomato
  • One cup of water (for boiling)
  • One spoon of grounded pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop your onions and tomato into thin slices.
  2. Pour one cup of water into a pot.
  3. Boil your egg in the water.
  4. After 5 minutes, remove your boiled egg.
  5. Put your Indomie noodles into the boiled water.
  6. Add the spice sachets, chopped onions, and pepper.
  7. Stir slightly and cover to cook for 3 minutes.
  8. Serve on a plate as shown above.
Chef's Tip: To make the dish richer, add extra spices and additional protein of your choice. Enjoy your meal!
