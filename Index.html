Simple Indomie recipe

Prep time - 5mins | cook time - 3 to 5mins | servings - 1 Plate | Difficulty-Easy peasy

Ingredients

  • One or two packets of indomie noodles (whichever flavor of your preference)
  • one or two eggs
  • one medium onion
  • one medium tomato
  • one cup of water - for boiling
  • one spoon of grounded pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop your onions and tomato to preferred thin slices.
  2. Pour your cup of water.
  3. Boil your egg in the water.
  4. After 5 mins, remove your boiled egg.
  5. Put in your indomie noodle in the boiled water.
  6. Add the spice satchets, chopped onions and a spoon of grounded pepper.
  7. Stir slightly and cover to cook for 3minutes
  8. After cooked to desired texture, on a plate as shown above
Chef's Tip; To make the dish richer, you can add the spices of your choosing and add more protein of preference. Hope you Enjoy your meal!
